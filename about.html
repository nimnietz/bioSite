A Bit about Dawn

Dawn grew up in a quiet town, where her love for reading blossomed into a passion for writing, particularly in the sci-fi, fantasy, and horror genres. She draws inspiration from her vivid imagination and personal experiences.

In addition to writing, Dawn enjoys baking, a hobby she developed while helping her mother in the kitchen during childhood. Professionally, she values the diversity of people she meets, as connecting with individuals from various backgrounds enriches her understanding of different perspectives.

Her friends encourage her to explore new interests, enhancing her life experiences. In the future, Dawn aims to publish a work of fiction, start a bakery, or share her crafts online. A travel enthusiast, she cherishes memories from Rainier Park in Washington and aspires to explore Europe and Asia.

Guided by values of authenticity and joy, she embraces each step of her journey with an adventurous spirit.
